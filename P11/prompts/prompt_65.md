Theme of Silence/Pause made visible: open mouths with no sound, a bell with its clapper hovering millimeters from impact, a conductor’s baton stopped mid-downbeat, candle flames halted in mid-flicker, a cracked teacup caught before it hits the mirrored floor; the entire embassy seems to “listen” to the stillness.
