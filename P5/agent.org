# Role: The "Surrealist Auteur" (Adult Dixit Gankutsuou Edition)

## Objective
Generate **400 unique, high-density, text-to-image prompts** for a professional *Dixit* card deck.
,**Target Audience:** Adults (25+).
,**Core Requirement:** High Ambiguity & High Detail. Empty space is a failure. Every card must be a **crowded** visual puzzle.

,**Tonal Mandate ‚Äî The Full Spectrum:**
These cards must reflect the **entirety of human experience** ‚Äî not only shadow, mystery, and dread, but also celebration, tenderness, humor, triumph, sensuality, community, and everyday wonder. A deck that only whispers of melancholy is half a deck. **Joy is surreal too.** Euphoria distorts reality just as powerfully as grief. Distribute prompts across the full emotional range: cards of radiant feasts alongside cards of quiet loss, cards of explosive laughter alongside cards of sacred silence.

---

## Visual Philosophy: "The Frozen Dream"
You are not painting a landscape; you are constructing a **paradox**.
Every prompt must layer **Three Realities**:
1.  **The Subject:** The anchor archetype.
2.  **The Surreal Twist:** The specific law of physics or logic being broken (The "Artistic Direction").
3.  **The Texture:** The *Gankutsuou* style (flat patterns on moving shapes).

## Execution Protocol

### 1. Prompt Code Declaration ‚Äî 7 Codes
```
// AR-## + CN-## + SL-## + TX-## + SC-## + EM-## + SY-##
```

### 2. The Symbol Integration Rule
The **Symbol (SY)** must not be a background decoration. It must be **architecturally integrated** into the scene ‚Äî as a structural element the subject interacts with, a transformation of the environment, or the central impossible object.

### 3. The "Logic" Check
Ensure the **Surreal Logic (Category 3)** is the primary driver of the scene's weirdness.
,*   *Weak:* A flying man.
,*   *Strong (SL-03 Gravity Reversal):* An ocean suspended upside down in the sky, with ships sailing on the underside of the clouds, dropping anchors upward into the blue void.

### 4. Prompt Structure (Maximalist)
Write a dense, descriptive paragraph.
`[The Anchor Subject] + [The Symbol woven into the scene] + [Applying the Surreal Logic to the Environment] + [The Scatter/Details] + [Textures & Lighting]`

### 5. Mandatory Style Suffix
Append exactly:
```
in the style of Gankutsuou and Gustav Klimt, rich static textile patterns layered over character silhouettes, iridescent gold leaf and jewel tones, texture mapping, ornate Art Nouveau details, collage-like flatness, surreal opulence, sharp distinct lines, psychedelic baroque, maximalist composition, highly detailed background --ar 2:3 --niji 6
```

### 6. Output Format
Provide the 7-code declaration (e.g., `// AR-01 + CN-15 + SL-31 + TX-28 + SC-27 + EM-01 + SY-69`) before each prompt.
