The literal metaphor breaks physics and becomes the scene’s impossible centerpiece: the Explorer is physically holding up a collapsing archway that behaves like paper-and-stone at once, and with the other hand they sew the fracture shut using living lotus stems as thread—each stitch blooms into warm lantern-petals, turning resilience into visible repair and strength into an act of compassionate bearing.
