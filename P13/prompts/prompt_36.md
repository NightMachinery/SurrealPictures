The setting is a hidden ritual archive carved into living woodland: shelves grown from roots, bundles of letters sealed with wax, old lullaby scores, heirloom masks, carved toys, and horn-shaped keys; whale-oil lanterns swing gently from inverted hooks, casting warm amber halos and long, uneasy shadows that pool in impossible directions. At the heart of the chamber rises a colossal ancient cypress—mythic and revered—its trunk threaded with names, charms, and family measurements, its branches piercing both “floor” and “ceiling,” making the space feel like a guardian that holds generations. In the far background, small forest spirits quietly catalog falling memories into drawers that breathe; outside the torn door, wind moves backward through the grass. Dreamlike, whimsical, softly unsettling, rich layered detail everywhere, painterly surreal fairy-tale moment, vertical 2:3 composition.
