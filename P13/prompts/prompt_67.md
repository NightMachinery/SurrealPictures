In the deep background: lantern-lit balconies crowded with silent masked spectators, dangling trapezes, frayed posters of forgotten acts, balloon rigging, stitched curtains fluttering, and far-off cable bridges fading into fog—everything richly textured, whimsical yet faintly unsettling, like a fairy tale paused mid-breath.
