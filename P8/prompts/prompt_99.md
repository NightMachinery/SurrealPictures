The theme of **abandonment/desolation** permeates the clutter: empty pedestals with dust outlines where masterpieces used to stand, torn accession ledgers, cracked glass cases, wilted laurel wreaths, missing nameplates, and a lone museum chair turned toward nothing—yet everything is held in tranquil suspension, as if the vault itself is quietly exhaling.
