A living burning bush erupts from a filing cabinet beside the Oracle—flames blooming as leaves and blossoms, casting mischievous, warm copper light that makes shadows grin; within the fire, tiny courtroom masks and scales flicker like spirits. Dream logic is blatant: shelves pour upward like waterfalls, flames float as solid petals, and suspended pages cast shadows that don’t match their shapes.
